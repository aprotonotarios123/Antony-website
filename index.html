About Me

Here's a short timeline of my life so far

  • 1994

    Expat Life

    On the 27th of November I was born in London, UK and I spent the next 18 years moving to Switzerland, South Africa, Indonesia and India!

  • 2013

    UCLA

    After finishing High School in Switzerland I made the move out to Los Angeles to study Economics & Entrepreneurship at UCLA.

  • 2014

    Catching the Startup Bug

    I spent my first summer in LA as a Data Analyst and Growth Hacker at a small startup in Santa Monica, and thats where I caught the bug and started my first company, ourWAV.

  • 2015

    VCs & Blackstone

    After interning for another startup that summer I spent the year of 2015 working at a local VC firm and at an oncampus accelerator run by Blackstone.

  • 2016

    IBM & Ryder

    My first real endeavour with tech came when I co-founded a tech startup out of my business class and worked as an Extreme Blue intern at IBM.

  • 2017

    BCG Digital Ventures

    This summer I will be working as a Venture Architect!
